Check Your Status

Look up your admission and fee payment status using your Admission ID, registered mobile number, or email.

This look-up calls GET /students/lookup?query= on the live Apps Script backend, which reads the Students / Payments_Log sheets directly — no separate login system, since the Admission ID + a matching phone/email is treated as sufficient proof of identity for a status check (not for making changes).
